Documentation

I currently work as a tech writer at Atlassian, on the Confluence design team. Contrary to popular belief, the majority of my purview is not writing documentation. What I actually do can be best summarised as 'making our software easier to understand.'

I'm intimately involved in the design process, participate in user testing, and work with a designer and product manager to refine flows. I know our customers well - I read feedback, participate in forums, etc so that I can adequately understand their needs and what they struggle with. And I know the product, so I can write about it.
